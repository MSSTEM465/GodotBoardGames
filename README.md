# GridGames

A collection of 6 grid-based games on **Godot 4.5.1**.

List of games:

- Nonograms
	- A simple yet challenging game where a board hides an image from the numbers at the side!
- Minesweeper
	- A classic pack-in computer game where numbers are the only way to avoid stepping on a mine!
- Reversi
	- Swap, swap, swap! Swap over your opponents disks to dominate the board!
- Knucklebones (Minigame from Cult of the Lamb)
	- A simple minigame from the video game Cult of the Lamb. Get lucky, skillfully sabatoge your opponent all-while gaining score!
- Battleship
	- A game of luck and educated guessing! Hope you strike the right tile, or you might sacrifice a ship..
- John Conway's Game of Life
	- A 0 player game, set up a board and watch as the cells grow and adapt!
